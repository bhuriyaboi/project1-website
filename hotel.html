Usha Kiran Palace


Address:Jayendra Ganj Rd, Roshni Ghar Mohalla, Lashkar, Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh 474009.
Phone:0751 244 4000

Set amidst 9 acres of beautifully landscaped gardens, Usha Kiran Palace Gwalior speaks of royal regalia and sophistication from every inch and corner of its beautiful architecture. Outfitted with artistic stone carvings and delicate filigree work, the establishment has previously played host to the King of England, and now stands tall as a heritage hotel. Managed by a very well established hotel chain, Usha Kiran Palace has stuck to great standards of hospitality making it one of the most sough after accommodation spaces of the city. Due to its fantastic location, which is in close proximity to key commercial and industrial areas, the place has been attracting a plethora of business and leisure travellers to its doorstep on a regular basis. Apart from housing 40 beautiful rooms, the hotel is also home to several recreational activities, various options for food and drinks, and fantastic meeting/banqueting facilities. One of the prime reasons behind the ever growing popularity of this structure has been its very warm and hospitable staff, who have been trained well enough to make sure that ever visitor who steps into the edifice goes back with fond memories of its local culture and exquisite architecture. Booking Usha Kiran Palace, Gwalior online has also been made possible by the hotel chain so as to make the whole process of reservations and accommodation simpler.
